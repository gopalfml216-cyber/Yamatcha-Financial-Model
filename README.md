# Yamatcha Restaurant Financial Model

A comprehensive 3-year financial model built in Microsoft Excel for **Yamatcha Restaurant**, designed to evaluate the financial feasibility of launching and operating a restaurant business.

The model follows industry-standard financial modeling practices and integrates operational assumptions with the three financial statements.

---

## Project Overview

This project simulates the financial performance of a restaurant from construction to operations by forecasting:

- Revenue
- Operating Expenses
- Profitability
- Working Capital
- Capital Expenditure
- Financing
- Cash Flows
- Balance Sheet

The model is completely dynamic, allowing users to modify assumptions and instantly observe their impact on financial performance.

---

## Features

### Assumptions Sheet
- Construction timeline
- Restaurant opening date
- Customer footfall assumptions
- Weekday & weekend demand
- Pricing assumptions
- Revenue growth
- Cost assumptions

---

### Revenue Model
Forecasts revenue across multiple product categories based on operational assumptions.

Includes:
- Customer volume
- Average selling prices
- Monthly revenue projections
- Growth assumptions

---

### Monthly Profit & Loss Statement

Projects monthly:

- Revenue
- Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
- Gross Profit
- Employee Expenses
- Operating Expenses
- EBITDA
- Depreciation
- Interest
- Profit Before Tax
- Net Profit

---

### Capital Structure

Models project financing through:

- Equity
- Debt
- Loan schedules
- Interest calculations

---

### Fixed Asset Schedule

Tracks:

- Capital expenditure
- Asset additions
- Depreciation
- Closing net block

---

### Working Capital Model

Forecasts:

- Inventory
- Receivables
- Payables
- Net Working Capital

---

### Annual Financial Statements

Automatically generates:

- Annual Profit & Loss Statement
- Balance Sheet
- Cash Flow Statement

All statements are fully linked and balanced.
